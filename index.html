Education

Technology University Dublin

My education in TUD encompassed a 4 year course in the Computing Program where I am to complete several modules per semester. These modules would then be graded and a GPA provided to access a student's performance with the minimum to pass a year being a GPA of 2.0 and a pass in all modules. I am currently in the middle of my 4th year in TUD without any fails to my name which shows me dedication to get a task done efficiently and effectively.

Adamstown Community College

In Adamstown, Students were divided up into 6 years. 1st - 3rd Year were the Juniors and 4th - 6th year were the seniors. The Junior part of the course is a preperation for the Leaving Cert as it has the students prepare for the Junior Cert which acts as an example as to what the Leaving Cert will be like. The 4th year of the course is an optional year and focuses and developing the students life skills such as planning out routes, cooking, building, etc. The 5th and 6th years have the students properly do their Leaving Cert which they can use to decide which college to go to if they wish to do so.

Griffeen Valley Educate Together

Griffeen Valley's system revolves around teaching the fundamentals of an academic course to young students who are ripe with potential. The system works on an 8 year progress starting with Junior Infants which is where infant students learn. They then become Senior infants a year later and steadily progress as the years go on. There is no real fear of being held back a year as the course is very lenient in how it approaches its grading system.

HSE Project

Calender App

In my On-Campus Internship, I was tasked with completing a project provided by the HSE corporation alongside 2 teammates. The problem we were given was to solve people not going to the hospital or taking their medication. Our solution to this was a calender application that would send out reminders to the user about their hospital appointments and other medical needs. We found this solution as we realised that while we cannot force someone to do these things we can remind them as people forgetting to do so is one of the most common ways for them to not do it.
